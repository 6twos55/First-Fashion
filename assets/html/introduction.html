Introduction

First Fashion sprouted from a school project and has now grown into one of the best shirt sites of the world, blossoming over all of Nigeria. We don't plan on killing this overwhelming yet transforming development, so we'll keep this journey ever interesting for all of us to enjoy.

Alot of hands and heads came together to make this a reality

About Us

Our mission drives us to do everything possible to expand human potential. We do that by creating groundbreaking shirt innovations, by making our products more sustainably, by building a creative and diverse global team and by making a positive impact in communities where we live and work.
